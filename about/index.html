Philosophy

To us, security is a crucial part of any business. We believe all businesses, regardless of size, should have access to affordable cybersecurity consultation. Our business model is built on that belief. No matter what industry you are in, our job is to provide affordable security services.

Origin

Our CEO Jeff spent years of his career focused on securing large corporations against various cyber threats. Through this, he realized most small businesses with a digital presence have vulnerable technological ecosystems without the proper help from security professionals. Jeff started the Keeber Security Group as a way to spread safe computing practices through all levels of the corporate world.
